How to use:

Enter a JavaScript regular expression (without the surrounding slashes) and optionally a seed number. Click "Generate Strings" to create random strings that match your pattern. The seed ensures reproducible results - the same seed will always generate the same strings.

Examples:

  • ^[a-z]{3,5}$ - generates lowercase words of 3-5 letters
  • ^\d{3}-\d{4}$ - generates phone number patterns like 555-1234
  • ^(cat|dog|bird)s?$ - generates animal names, singular or plural
